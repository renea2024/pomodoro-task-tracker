How to Use Pomo.Pop

Pomo.Pop is based on the Pomodoro Technique: 25 minutes of focused work followed by a 5-minute break to help you stay productive and refreshed.

  1. To Do: Click the + button to add new tasks. Click the ▶ icon to move a task to In Progress and automatically start the 25-minute timer.
  2. In Progress: Work on one task at a time. When you're done, click the ✔ icon to move it to the Complete column. This triggers Break Mode automatically.
  3. Complete: Your completed tasks live here. The timer will resume focus mode after the five minute break.
  4. Break Mode: While on break, the task board is blurred and task adding is disabled. Once the timer ends, focus mode resumes.

Organize, focus, and pop through your day.
